Search Engine Optimisation

Client · A wellness & specialty beverage brand
Technical SEO On-page Content Off-page CRO

The challenge

  • The website was struggling to reach the first page and gain organic search traffic.
  • The core objective was to rank for highly competitive terms such as caramel flavouring syrup, vegan dessert sauce, matcha powder and matcha tea.
  • Improving rankings was difficult given the crowded specialty-beverage niche in the target market.
  • Poor user interface and user experience were driving a high bounce rate.
  • Keyword targeting and page intent were unclear and inconsistent.

Bounce rateReduced

Our strategy

01

Website & keyword research to identify the top 100 keywords relevant to the industry.

02

On-page fixes — meta tags, XML sitemap, Search Console & analytics setup, and image optimisation.

03

Manual submissions to top search engines, plus article promotion, blogging and press releases.

04

Ad posting, business listings, Web 2.0 profile creation, product submissions and infographics.

05

Video & image promotion, review sharing, PDF & deck submissions and competitor analysis.

06

Conversion-rate optimisation — clearer CTAs and informational content across the homepage.

Achievements

  • Increased search ranking for highly competitive keywords.
  • Grew organic traffic month over month through targeted SEO.
  • Significantly reduced bounce rate by optimising UX and interface design.
  • Fixed core web-vital and user-experience issues.
  • Resolved technical SEO errors, including broken URLs.
  • Restructured website URLs for clarity, SEO and better navigation.

  • Low open rates (12%) due to weak subject-line optimisation.
  • High unsubscribe rate (5%) caused by irrelevant content.
  • Poor click-through rate (1.8%) from a lack of personalisation.
  • Compliance demands specific to financial-services email marketing.

Objectives

  • Increase email open rates and CTR.
  • Reduce unsubscribes with value-driven content.
  • Improve segmentation and personalisation.
  • Stay compliant with financial-marketing regulations.
